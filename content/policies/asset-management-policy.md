# ISMS-DOC-S01-01 Asset Management

## 1 Introduction

This policy outlines the procedures for managing and tracking company assets to ensure accountability, security, and efficient utilization.

## 2 Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who are issued company-owned devices or have access to company assets.

**Asset Tracking:**

- All hardware and software assets are recorded in the company's Asset Register.
- Devices and software are managed and monitored through Microsoft Intune to ensure compliance with security and usage policies.

**Responsibilities:**

- Employees are responsible for the proper use and care of any assigned assets.
- Any loss, theft, or damage must be reported immediately to the IT department.
- IT is responsible for updating the Asset Register and managing devices via Intune.

**Asset Lifecycle:**

- Assets must be returned to IT for proper decommissioning or reassignment upon termination of employment or when no longer needed.
- Periodic audits will be conducted to verify the location and condition of assets.

## 3 Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

## 4 Review

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect changes in technology or business needs.
